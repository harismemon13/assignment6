MOUNTAIN

What are Mountains?

Mountains are natural elevations of the earth's surface, formed by geological processes such as volcanic activity and plate tectonics.

Types of Mountains

Mountains can be categorized into different types, including fold mountains, volcanic mountains, and block mountains.

Benefits of Mountains

Mountains provide several benefits, including habitat for diverse wildlife, source of fresh water, and opportunities for recreation and tourism.

Best Places to Visit Mountains

Some of the best places to visit mountains include the Himalayas, the Rocky Mountains, and the Swiss Alps.

Tips for Hiking in Mountains

To hike safely in mountains, follow these tips: wear proper gear and clothing, bring plenty of water and snacks, and follow marked trails and guides.
