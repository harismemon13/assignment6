SERIVCE

Jazz ke dwara pradaan ki jaane wali services mein shamil hain:

  1. Mobile Phone Services: Jazz Pakistan mein mobile phone services pradaan karti hai, jismein call, SMS, aur internet services shamil hain.
  2. Internet Services: Jazz broadband aur mobile internet services pradaan karti hai, jisse log apne ghar, office, ya mobile device par internet access kar sakte hain.
  3. Digital Solutions: Jazz digital solutions pradaan karti hai, jismein online payment, mobile banking, aur e-commerce solutions shamil hain.
