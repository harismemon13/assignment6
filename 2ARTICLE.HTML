ARTICLE 2:BEACH



What are Beaches?

Beaches are coastal landforms that consist of loose sediment, such as sand, pebbles, or rocks.

Types of Beaches

Beaches can be categorized into different types, including sandy beaches, rocky beaches, and pebble beaches.

Benefits of Beaches

Beaches provide several benefits, including opportunities for recreation and relaxation, habitat for diverse marine life, and source of income for local communities.

Best Places to Visit Beaches

Some of the best places to visit beaches include Hawaii, California, and Florida.

Tips for Visiting Beaches

To visit beaches safely, follow these tips: wear sunscreen and protective clothing, swim in designated areas, and respect local marine life and habitats.
